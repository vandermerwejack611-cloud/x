About Me

My love for technology, robots and artificial intelligence started at an early age, growing up with movies such as the Matrix, Bicentennial Man and Minority Report. After school, I got the opportunity to work at a company that inspects mining machinery, and studied to become an NDT (Non-destructive testing) technician, which fueled my passion for technology. Life happens, and the past 8 years I found myself in Pretoria, where I used every spare moment to learn and teach myself website coding and development. I don't want to be the next Elon, I just want to build the next best agentic systems and digital solutions for real clients with real-world problems.
